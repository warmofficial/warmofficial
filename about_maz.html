About Us: Mazal

Meet the Creators

Mazal

Mazal is currently sixteen years-old and a rising high school seniors. Her hobbies are reading fiction, writing short stories, and drawing. She has always been interested in pursuing a career in STEM, specifically medicine; however, she chose to do Girls Who Code to expand her interests and now is considering a career in biomedical engineering due to how much she enjoys coding.
Mazal has always felt the importance of mental health issues. She feels that something as widespread as this should not be stigmatized nor ignored. Many people that she knows suffer from some mental health issues and refuse to get help because they are worried about others hearing about it. This is why she felt that this game would be important: it's bringing awareness, it's normalizing this issue.
