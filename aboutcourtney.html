Meet the Creators

Courtney is a junior in Francis Lewis High School. She is very enthusiastic about the Computer Science field due to her exposure and love for video games as well as growing up around the internet. She is very involved in her school's Patriot Battlion for Junior Reserve Officer Training Corps (JROTC) and is learning about leadership and teamwork. Some of her other hobbies include playing the piano and photography.

Courtney and two others created "Warm" to as a way to advocate for mental health. As a young adult growing up with a lot of cognizance and information about mental health she believes in spreading knowledge and awareness about the issue. By creating this game she hopes to achieve spreaing awareness and that it will educate other people about mental health issues.
